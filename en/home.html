Decidim's 10 assets

  • Customizable and modular to adapt to your context.
  • Numerous functionalities to animate your participatory budgets, public consultations, etc.
  • Approaches managed by administrators and sometimes initiated by participants (petitions).
  • Enhanced social dimension with personalized notifications, subscription profiles and private messaging between users.
  • Advanced administration panel that helps you organize your work.
  • Fine management of administration rights.
  • Available in SAAS.
  • Comprehensive API for open data.
  • Truly open source.
  • Compliant with GDPR.
